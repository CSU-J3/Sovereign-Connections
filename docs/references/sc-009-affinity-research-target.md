## The nexus, firm-level — primary-sourced from Affinity's own ADV

- **Ownership:** A Fin Management LLC ("Affinity") is a Delaware LLC founded 2021, offices in Sunny Isles Beach FL. **Sole owner: Jared Kushner.** (ADV Part 2A, Item 4.A.)
- **Scale:** AUM as of 2025-12-31 is **$6,160,297,411**, all discretionary. (ADV Part 2A, Item 4.E.) Up from ~$4.8B at end-2024 and ~$3.06B at end-2022 — a rising trajectory.
- **Fee structure (what the capital generates for Kushner):** **2.0% annualized management fee** (0.5% quarterly) plus performance fee/carry of **up to 20%** of realized proceeds. (ADV Part 2A, Items 5.A. and 6.) On ~$6.16B AUM the management fee alone is on the order of ~$120M/year before carry.
- **Sovereign nexus, in Affinity's own words:** the ADV lists the client/investor categories for its Private Funds as including **"sovereign wealth funds."** (ADV Part 2A, Item 7.) This is the sovereign-source nexus confirmed in the *primary* record — Affinity itself discloses sovereign-wealth-fund capital among its investors. Minimum investment is generally **$25M–$50M**.

## Drift guards — load-bearing

- **No payback / causation claim.** The oversight letters (Maloney, Wyden) frame PIF's investment as potential payback for Kushner's official acts (his MBS relationship as a WH senior adviser). That is **drift mode 1** (payment occurred → payment caused outcome) and is denied. The record documents: sovereign funds placed billions into a vehicle wholly owned by a former senior official who had a close relationship with the controlling principal while in office, and that vehicle now manages $6.16B at 2%+20%. **It stops there.**
- **The drift guard is *easy* to hold here** precisely because Kushner held no office during the reporting period (left government Jan 2021; Affinity incorporated the day after). There is no contemporaneous official act to causally link — which is itself worth stating in the record as the reason the relationship is documented without a causal assertion.
- **Three distinct sovereign vehicles.** PIF, QIA, Lunate — separate funds, separate governance. Catalog and cite each individually; never "the Gulf" as a unit.
- **ADV categories ≠ named investors.** The primary record confirms *sovereign-wealth-fund capital is present*; it does not itself name PIF/QIA/Lunate. Keep the firm-level facts (primary) and the named-fund facts (secondary) on separate evidentiary footing in the record. Do not let the richness of the secondary reporting silently upgrade the named-fund facts to primary.
- **EA acquisition (2025-26):** Affinity was in the consortium (PIF-controlled, Silver Lake third) that acquired Electronic Arts; Kushner reportedly introduced PIF to EA. Document as business development if included at all. He held no office in 2025-26 — no official act to link.
